📅 Study Plan 12 Minggu

Target: Band 6.5 · 1.5 jam/hari

Fase 1 — Foundation (Minggu 1–4)

Bangun dasar grammar, vocabulary akademik, teknik dasar tiap section.

Minggu 1–2
Grammar dasar · 1 passage Reading no-timer · Listening Section 1–2 · Paragraf Writing
Minggu 3–4
Reading dengan timer 20 menit · Speaking Part 1 rekam diri · 10 collocations/hari

Fase 2 — Skill Building (Minggu 5–8)

Naikkan akurasi & kompleksitas. Mulai full essay & full speaking test.

Minggu 5–6
Full essay Task 1 & 2 · Listening Section 3–4 · Speaking full test mingguan
Minggu 7–8
AI feedback per esai · error log tracking · academic phrases

Fase 3 — Test Practice (Minggu 9–12)

Simulasi penuh, fix kelemahan spesifik, naik ke band 6.5.

Minggu 9–10
Section drill harian · Full mock test mingguan · shadowing TED Talk
Minggu 11–12
Mock test 2x/minggu · Fix pola error · Final review
